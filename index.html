What is the natural logarithm?
The natural logarithm of a specific number is it's logarithm to the base of the mathematical constant e. Which is an irrational and transcendental number approximately equal to 2.71828.
How to calculate natural logarithm?
To calculate natural logarithm you should use the following formula:
e^lnx = x
where:
ln = natural logarithm
e = natural exponent
x = real number
Why use natural logarithm?
The natural logarithm is usually used in order ot solve time and growth problems.
